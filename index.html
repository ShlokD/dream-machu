Popular Questions

WHAT IS MACHU PICCHU Machu Picchu is an ancient Inca city located in the Andes Mountains of Peru. It was built in the 15th century and was abandoned shortly after the Spanish conquest of Peru. Machu Picchu is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world, and it was declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 1983.
WHEN IS THE BEST TIME TO VISIT The best time to visit Machu Picchu is during the dry season, which runs from April to October. However, Machu Picchu is a popular destination year-round, so it is important to book your tour in advance.
WHAT ARE THE RULES TO VISIT MACHU PICCHU You should pack comfortable walking shoes, sunscreen, a hat, sunglasses, and a raincoat. You may also want to pack a backpack to carry your belongings.
WHAT ARE THE RULES FOR VISITING There are a few rules for visiting Machu Picchu. You must have a valid ticket to enter the site, and you must be accompanied by a guide if you are visiting during the busy season. You are also not allowed to climb on the ruins or take any objects from the site.
